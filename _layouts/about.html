About Me

Ph.D. Student @ UC Berkeley
I'm a first-year PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Ren Ng and supported by the NSF GRFP. My research interests lie in the intersection of human vision and computer vision, as well as understanding color. Prior to arriving at Berkeley, I did my Bachelor's in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Deva Ramanan and mentored by Rohit Girdhar. Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, backpacking, photography, making latte art, and playing guitar!

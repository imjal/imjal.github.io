About Me

Ph.D. Student @ UC Berkeley
I'm a first-year EECS PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Ren Ng and supported by the NSF GRFP. My research interests lie in broadly in visual computing, but currently focused on the emergence of color vision. Prior to arriving at Berkeley, I did my Bachelor's in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Deva Ramanan and mentored by Rohit Girdhar. Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, backpacking, photography, making latte art, and listening to podcasts on the social sciences!

About Me

I'm a junior studying Computer Science at CMU. My research interests lie in meta-learning and generative modeling, and more broadly, generalization. At CMU, I'm working with Rohit Girdhar and Deva Ramanan on few-shot generative modeling for the pose-to-image generation task. Prior to that, I worked on determining fire risk for commercial properties in Pittsburgh, where a team and I built a pipeline for the fire inspectors to better route their inspections.
